Abstract

The Smart Crop Advisory System for Small and Marginal Farmers is a virtual decision-support platform designed to enhance agricultural practices without the need for costly hardware. The system uses data analytics, weather reports, soil type information, and crop patterns to generate customized recommendations for crop selection, fertilizer application, irrigation planning, and pest control. By providing user-friendly access through web or mobile platforms in local languages, it empowers farmers to make informed decisions at minimal cost. This approach reduces risks, optimizes resources, and ensures sustainable farming, thereby improving productivity and profitability for small and marginal farmers.
